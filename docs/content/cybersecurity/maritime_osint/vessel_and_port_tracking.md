# Category 1: Vessel and Ports Facility Tracking

## Overview
The ability to monitor vessel movements and maritime traffic constitutes a foundational capability within maritime OSINT. This category provides the essential data layer for establishing the target's operational baseline, including its real-time location, historical routes, speed, and port call patterns.

## AIS & Vessel Tracking Platforms
1.  **[MarineTraffic](https://www.marinetraffic.com/)** - The standard for real-time vessel tracking using AIS data.
2.  **[VesselFinder](https://www.vesselfinder.com/)** - Free real-time AIS vessel tracking map.
3.  **[FleetMon](https://www.fleetmon.com/)** - Vessel tracking and maritime news.
4.  **[ShipFinder](https://www.shipfinder.com/)** - Live ship tracking application.
5.  **[VT Explorer](https://www.vtexplorer.com/)** - Vessel tracking and maritime analytics.
6.  **[MyShipTracking](https://www.myshiptracking.com/)** - Real-time vessel tracking and monitoring.
7.  **[CruiseMapper](https://www.cruisemapper.com/)** - Specific tracking for cruise ships and itineraries.
8.  **[TankerTrackers](https://www.tankertrackers.com/)** - Monitoring oil tanker movements and floating storage.
9.  **[MarineCadastre.gov](https://marinecadastre.gov/)** - US-based AIS data for research and planning.
10. **[UN Global Platform](https://unstats.un.org/UNSD/energy/statistics/ais/)** - AIS data for sustainable development.

## Port Operations & Logistics
11. **[Port of Rotterdam Map](https://www.portofrotterdam.com/en/shipping/ship-traffic/map)** - Real-time vessel movements in the Port of Rotterdam.
12. **[Portmaps](https://portmaps.com/)** - Interactive map of ports and terminals.
13. **[Container Tracking](https://www.container-tracking.org/)** - Track containers by number across different lines.
14. **[SeaRates](https://www.searates.com/)** - Container tracking and logistics tools.
15. **[PortVision](https://portvision.com/)** - AIS vessel tracking and waterway management.
16. **[CargoSmart](https://www.cargosmart.com/)** - Shipment tracking and analytics.
