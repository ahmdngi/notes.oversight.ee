# Category 2: Administrative and Regulatory Maritime Intelligence

## Overview
These resources answer the critical questions of *who* controls a vessel, *how* it is managed, and its operational standing. This is essential for exposing complex ownership structures and sanctions evasion.

## Corporate & Ownership Registries
1.  **[Equasis](https://www.equasis.org/)** - The primary global database for ship ownership, management, and safety records.
2.  **[IMO GISIS](https://gisis.imo.org/)** - International Maritime Organization's Global Integrated Shipping Information System.
3.  **[OpenCorporates](https://opencorporates.com/)** - The largest open database of corporate entities, useful for tracing ownership chains.
4.  **[IHS Markit](https://maritime.ihs.com/)** - Industry standard for ship characteristics and ownership data (Commercial).
5.  **[Lloyd's List Intelligence](https://lloydslist.maritimeintelligence.informa.com/)** - Premium data on vessel movements and ownership.
6.  **[Bureau Veritas](https://www.veristar.com/)** - Class society registry for vessel classification status.
7.  **[DNV GL Vessel Register](https://vesselregister.dnvgl.com/)** - Database of DNV classed vessels.

## Sanctions & Compliance
8.  **[OFAC Sanctions List](https://sanctionssearch.ofac.treas.gov/)** - US Treasury Department sanctions screening.
9.  **[UN Security Council Consolidated List](https://www.un.org/securitycouncil/content/un-security-council-consolidated-list)** - UN sanctions list.
10. **[EU Sanctions Map](https://www.sanctionsmap.eu/)** - Map-based view of EU sanctions regimes.
11. **[C4ADS](https://c4ads.org/)** - Non-profit specializing in tracking illicit maritime networks.
12. **[Windward](https://windward.ai/)** - AI-driven maritime risk analytics.

## Website & Domain Recon
13. **[DNSDumpster](https://dnsdumpster.com/)** - DNS recon tool to map the digital infrastructure of shipping companies.
14. **[NSLookup.io](https://www.nslookup.io/)** - DNS record lookup for verifying maritime server infrastructure.
15. **[DNSTwist](https://github.com/elceef/dnstwist)** - Detect typosquatting domains targeting maritime brands.
16. **[DorkGPT](https://www.dorkgpt.com/)** - AI-powered search query generator to find exposed maritime documents.
17. **[SecurityTrails](https://securitytrails.com/)** - Historical DNS data for maritime assets.
18. **[Crt.sh](https://crt.sh/)** - Certificate transparency logs to find subdomains of shipping lines.
