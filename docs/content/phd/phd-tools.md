 
### **Research Tools**
- **[Publish-or-Perish](https://harzing.com/resources/publish-or-perish)** - Analyzes academic citations to measure research impact.  
- **[Research Rabbit](https://researchrabbitapp.com/home)** - Helps discover and organize academic papers efficiently.  
- **[Strike Plagiarism](https://panel.strikeplagiarism.com/)** - Detects and prevents plagiarism in academic and professional work.  
- **[Quilbot](https://quillbot.com/)** - A paraphrasing and grammar-checking tool for better writing.  
- **[Mindmeister](https://www.mindmeister.com/app/maps/team)** - A mind-mapping tool for brainstorming and organizing ideas.  
- **[Vosviewer](https://www.vosviewer.com/download)** - Visualizes scientific research networks and bibliometric data.  

### AI Tools  
- **[Elicit AI](https://elicit.com/)** - Uses AI to summarize and extract insights from research papers.  
- **[Google Noebook LM](https://notebooklm.google.com/)** - An AI-powered notebook for organizing and synthesizing notes.  
- **[Yomu AI](https://www.yomu.ai/)** - Assists with academic writing and editing.  
- **[Scite_AI](https://scite.ai/)** - Provides smart citations to verify research claims.  
- **[Rayyan AI](https://new.rayyan.ai/)** - AI-powered tool for systematic literature reviews.  
- **[Jenni AI](https://app.jenni.ai/)** - Enhances academic writing with AI-generated suggestions.  
- **[Statista AI](https://www.statista.com/research-ai/tool)** - Delivers data-driven insights and statistics using AI.  
- **[Scopus AI](https://www.scopus.com/search/form.uri#scopus-ai)** - AI-enhanced research discovery and analytics tool.
